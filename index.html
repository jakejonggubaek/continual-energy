Our Values

Transparency

Building solutions should not be a mystery to owners, contractors, engineers and operators. We believe that all stakeholders should be able to understand 'how it works' and access the technology. This is one of the reasons that we provide in depth owner training for all projects.

Accuracy

Striving for accuracy (both internally and externally) will help our customers achieve operational excellence, improve the quality of our products and services and deliver a competitive advantage for our customers and our company. This is one of the reasons that we offer performance guarantees.

Collaboration

Achieving and sustaining desired results relies on close collaboration with our customers. We view this collaboration as a partnership where desired project goals are shared - all parties moving in the same direction, together. This is one of the reasons that Continual provides in depth detailed analyses that look beyond initial project phases, provides continuous support and commissioning and supports projects for the life of the products and provides 24/7 support services.

Culture

Solving building challenges through innovative technology and engineering services requires transparency, accuracy and collaboration. This approach is sustaining results across various energy and building applications. Continual is helping to solve customer challenges and needs more reliably and effectively.

Products

Exhaustive Search Optimization Program

ExSOP

Exhaustive Search Optimization Program

Providing optimal HVAC system performance and control through the use of an exhaustive search algorithm, identifying the combination of setpoints, equipment sequences and conditioned-space parameters that result in the lowest energy input and/or lowest instantaneous cost of production.

Click the button to view the brochure, or view the ExSOP White Paper for more in depth information.

Philip Kennedy

Philip is an international and industry proven innovator and leader of HVAC design and system optimization. He has over 20 years of experience in central plant optimization and design, industrial automation, measurement and verification, variable speed centrifugal compressor and large capacity chiller development and implementation.

Philip's past success stories include the development of award winning central plant optimization platforms, the deployment of over 100 central plant optimization projects around the world, development of several innovative centrifugal chiller designs and controls currently utilized by major manufacturers, AHRI approved chiller performance test labs across North America and held a lead role in the development of the revolutionary Turbocor magnetic bearing variable speed compressor.

Philip has a deep interest in energy product development, thermal energy modeling, energy performance simulation technologies and software development. He is active with several ASHRAE Society committees on energy efficiency.

Philip studied HVAC engineering at Box Hill Institute in Australia where he gained several national and industry awards and also studied engineering at Royal Melbourne Institute of Technology.
