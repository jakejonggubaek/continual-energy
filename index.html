Our Values

Transparency

Building solutions should not be a mystery to owners, contractors, engineers and operators. We believe that all stakeholders should be able to understand 'how it works' and access the technology. This is one of the reasons that we provide in depth owner training for all projects.

Accuracy

Striving for accuracy (both internally and externally) will help our customers achieve operational excellence, improve the quality of our products and services and deliver a competitive advantage for our customers and our company. This is one of the reasons that we offer performance guarantees.

Collaboration

Achieving and sustaining desired results relies on close collaboration with our customers. We view this collaboration as a partnership where desired project goals are shared - all parties moving in the same direction, together. This is one of the reasons that Continual provides in depth detailed analyses that look beyond initial project phases, provides continuous support and commissioning and supports projects for the life of the products and provides 24/7 support services.

Culture

Solving building challenges through innovative technology and engineering services requires transparency, accuracy and collaboration. This approach is sustaining results across various energy and building applications. Continual is helping to solve customer challenges and needs more reliably and effectively.
